Wireframes are intentionally low-fidelity — they lock down layout, hierarchy, and flow before visual design. Every screen annotation references a functional requirement in PRD.md. Not yet shown (deferred / secondary): search results, trash & restore, account/settings, offline sync queue, and per-drive picker — all specified in the PRD but out of the core happy path.

Next step options: high-fidelity visual design of these screens, a clickable prototype flow, or the deferred secondary screens.
